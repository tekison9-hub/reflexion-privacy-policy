# Reflexion-privacy-policy                                                                                                                                  
Last updated: December 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how Reflexion (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user data.
Reflexion is a mobile game developed for entertainment purposes.
The App does not collect personally identifiable information (PII).

1. Data Collected

The App may collect the following non-personal data:

a) Device Information
Device model
Operating system version
Performance metrics
This data is used only to improve gameplay stability and performance.

b) In-Game Analytics
The App may process:
Level progression
High scores
Gameplay events
This information helps improve game balance and user experience.

c) Advertising Data
If third-party ad services (e.g., Google AdMob) are used, they may collect:
Approximate location
Device identifiers
Interaction signals with ads
Data collection is handled by Google’s ad systems and follows Google’s policies.

The App does not store or process this data directly.

2. Information NOT Collected
The App does NOT collect:

Name
Email
Phone number
Address
Contacts
Photos
Precise location
Payment information
Any sensitive personal data

3. How Data Is Used

Collected data is used only for:
Improving gameplay
Crash diagnostics
Analytics
Delivering non-personalized or contextual ads

The App does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use:
Google AdMob (ads)
Google Play Services (analytics, diagnostics)
These services have their own privacy policies:
Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13.
No personal data is knowingly collected from children.

6. Security

All collected data (non-personal) is protected through Google’s standard mechanisms.
No personal information is stored by the developer.
